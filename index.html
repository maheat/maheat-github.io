About me

Developer & Designer

Welcome to my creative world! I'm Maheen Eatazaz, a passionate artist and photographer on a journey to capture the beauty of the world through my lens and artistic expressions.

Originally from Islamabad, Pakistan, I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD). While my academic journey in computer science is thriving, my heart truly belongs to the world of art and photography.

My artistic journey began during my childhood, where I developed a deep appreciation for various art forms. After experimenting with diverse mediums, I discovered my primary interest in creating nature-inspired paintings with acrylic and gouache. My passion for photography, a more recent endeavor, is an ongoing exploration where I continuously discover new angles and styles.

During my tenure at NYUAD, I've had the privilege of working on exciting projects related to machine learning and artificial intelligence. While these technical skills are an essential part of my academic growth, my love for art and photography remains at the core of my identity.
