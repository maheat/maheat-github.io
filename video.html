Arts Centre / مركزالفنون

Inspiration from the Heart of Creativity

The university's Arts Center holds a special place in my artistic journey. It's not just a building; it's the heart of creativity that has fueled my passion for the arts. As a student of interactive media courses, I found myself immersed in the world of technology and design.

However, it was the vibrant atmosphere of the Arts Center that rekindled my love for traditional art forms. Surrounded by fellow artists and their incredible works, I rediscovered the beauty of painting and photography. The Arts Center became my muse, and the interactive media courses inspired me to incorporate videography into my creative arsenal. This fusion of traditional and digital artistry is evident in my portfolio, where you'll find a tapestry of my artistic evolution, each piece reflecting the profound influence of this remarkable cener..
